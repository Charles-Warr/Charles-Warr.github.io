About Us

Welcome to Munnuo Games Studio – where the hustle is real and every pixel tells a story.

We’re the creative force behind Da Streetz, a gritty, immersive fighting RPG where survival isn’t a choice—it’s a lifestyle. Born from our love of street culture, anime aesthetics, and high-energy combat mechanics, our studio is driven by one goal: to build a world that feels alive, raw, and ready to be fought for.

Built using Unity and powered by Visual Studio, we fuse rapid iteration with polished performance to deliver a game that’s optimized for both mobile and desktop platforms.

What We Stand For

  • Street culture meets next-gen design – We honor the grind with cutting-edge visuals and systems.
  • Character and identity – Every fighter has a backstory, every street has a voice.
  • Creative control – From the move sets to your crib, we put customization front and center.
  • Community-first – Feedback fuels our growth. You shape Da Streetz as much as we do.
